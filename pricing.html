Frequently asked questions

Can I really build a complete app without coding?
Absolutely. caveman generates production-ready code from your natural language descriptions. You describe what you want — user flows, features, design preferences — and the AI handles the frontend, backend, database, and deployment. Thousands of non-technical founders have already launched real products.
What happens if I outgrow caveman?
You own your code. On Pro and Enterprise plans, you can export the full source code at any time and continue development with your own engineering team. There's no lock-in — we want you to succeed whether you stay with us or not.
How is this different from no-code tools like Bubble?
No-code tools give you a visual builder with constraints. caveman generates actual code with no limitations on logic or complexity. You get the speed of no-code with the power of custom development — and you can always export and own your codebase.
Can I cancel or change plans anytime?
Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your dashboard. If you cancel a paid plan, you keep access through the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees, no questions asked.
